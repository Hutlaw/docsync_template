World

Characters

        Elric Alphonse

The Main Villain

A genius, a scientist, an inventor

After being declared insane, he breaks free from the asylum. He deems humanity (and the other species) unworthy and starts off on a quest to exterminate the “virus” plaguing the place. He leaves puzzles around the city to challenge the individuals. After the party bests his puzzles and fights off his henchman, the party starts closing in on him. As the party keeps beating Elric, he slowly grows to respect the party challenging him.

Puzzle ideas

The Dead Man’s Hand

A puzzle with a skeleton hand on a pedestal. The hand shall contain 5 differently colored gems, each representing something. There should be a sign or such nearby saying “One touch will save, one will kill, but heed this warning: Only a fool will take them all.” After deciphering the hidden representation of the colors of the stones, you should grab the right one from the hand, either unlocking the door or such. If the wrong stone is grabbed, CONSEQUENCES.
